Frequently Asked Questions: What kind of power outlets are used there?: The power outlets are 120 volts(same as the U.S.)
What are the laws regarding alcohol?: Alcohol is not allowed to be served between the hours of midnight and 9 am, the legal drinking age is 18 years old
What is the language spoken there?: Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, but in more rural areas it can be hard to find someone that speaks english, The hospital has many multilingual employees so you don thave to worry if there is an emergency
When is a good time to visit the island?: You can come anytime but for most national holidays many attractions and restaurants will be closed
What currency is use there?: Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency but many businesses will accept euros and yen, we have several banks that facillitate currency exchanges.

Public Transportation: Public buses run from 5 am to 11 pm everyday in the city, Private buses serve the rest of the island. taxis are also available and they have rental cars that can be rented by the airport at the local rental angency Bikes and helmets can ber rented from several vendors(helmets are required by law).
