If you aren't relaxing on our beautiful beach, exploring our lush rainforest, or hiking our active volcano, there are many other forms of entertainment we have here. We offer chartered fishing tours, zip-lining, and helicopter rides. We also have a museum detailing the islands history, a microbrewery you can visit, dance clubs, movies, arcades, and so much more!
